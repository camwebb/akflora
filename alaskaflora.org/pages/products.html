Project products and related resources

Project outcomes

  • Cam led a public discussion on the Taxon Concepts problem at the 2nd Annual Digital Data in Biodiversity Research Conference (2018)
  • During proposal development, Cam dafted a taxon concept relationship extension for Darwin Core Archives.
  • The development blog
  • A checklist of Alaskan plant names, highlighting conflicts in accepted status among source lists (ALA Checklist, the Panarctic Flora, Flora of North America, The Kew World Checklist of Selected Plant Families, and the UAA Checklist for the Flora of Alaska).
  • Hultén’s Flora of Alaska digitized
